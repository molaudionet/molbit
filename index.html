Chemistry Rules

  • Valence Rules:
    • Hydrogen (H): 1 bond
    • Carbon (C): 4 bonds
    • Nitrogen (N): 3 bonds
    • Oxygen (O): 2 bonds
    • Phosphorus (P): 5 bonds
    • Sulfur (S): 6 bonds
  • Stability: Molecules are stable when all atoms satisfy their valence requirements
  • Bonding: Double bonds count as 2, triple bonds count as 3
  • Overbonding: Adding more bonds than valence allows reduces stability!
